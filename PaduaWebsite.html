Padua Academy Visual Arts

Padua Academy's Visual Arts program has many different classes to choose from including Introduction to Art, Art 1, 2, 3, and 4, AP Art, and Graphic Design.

These classes and extra curricular activities allow Padua students to express themselves through their art, magazines, or advertisements. Our staff is fantastic in helping tudents gain confidence and creativity when they take one of these courses.
